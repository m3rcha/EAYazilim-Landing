# Product Context

## Purpose
EA Yazılım POS software needs a professional online presence to attract new B2B customers, clearly explain its value proposition, and generate leads for its POS solutions.

## Problems Solved
- Communicates the benefits of modern POS solutions: easy to use, 24/7 active, local operation support (offline capabilities for lifetime licenses), and suitability for various business sizes.
- Transparently presents a base subscription price (900₺/month) with clearly priced add-on modules so clients can customize their package and understand costs.
- Builds trust through a clear FAQ section, accessible contact methods (phone, email, WhatsApp), and transparent legal documentation.

## User Experience Goals
- Fast, responsive, and visually appealing minimalist design.
- Clear call-to-actions (e.g., "İletişime Geç", "Fiyatları İncele") to guide users towards making contact.
- Easy and intuitive navigation between product features, pricing, and contact sections.
- Seamless presentation of legal policies in the footer for credibility.
