# Project Brief

## EA Yazılım Website

**Description**: 
Landing page and static website for EA Yazılım, a company developing modern POS (Point of Sale) applications. 

**Core Requirements & Goals**:
- Provide a high-conversion, minimalist landing page for EA Yazılım POS software.
- Showcase the software's features, target sectors (cafes, restaurants, chain branches), and transparent pricing tiers (Başlangıç, Pro, Enterprise).
- Provide business contact information and integrated lead generation forms.
- Ensure legal compliance and build trust with dedicated pages for Terms of Service (Kullanım Koşulları), Privacy Policy (Gizlilik Politikası), KVKK Disclosure (KVKK Aydınlatma Metni), and Refund Policy (İade Politikası).
- Implement a secure admin panel (`admin.eayazilim.tr`) to handle contact form submissions, incorporating authentication and super-admin user management (RBAC).
